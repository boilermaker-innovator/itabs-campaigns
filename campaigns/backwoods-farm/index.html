Introducing: Backwoods by Kettle Ridge

What is it?

Backwoods is an outdoor music venue in upstate New York, created by the family behind Kettle Ridge Farm. They're building a community space for live music, local food, and good times - all set among the beautiful wooded hills of Victor, NY.

Why back this?

Help a local family farm expand into something special. They've already built a loyal following with 10,000+ fans in just one month. Your backing supports live music, local food, and a gathering place that brings the community together.

How does it work?

Choose a pledge tier to support the venue. Rewards include farm products, event tickets, and exclusive experiences at Backwoods. The team at Kettle Ridge has been running successful farm events since 2010 - they know how to deliver.

What makes it special?

It's not just a venue - it's a farm-to-table experience. The same family that makes award-winning maple syrup (featured in Vogue, Forbes, Food & Wine) is bringing that quality to outdoor entertainment. Real community, real food, real music.
