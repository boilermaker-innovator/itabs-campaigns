Sky Nexus

What is it?

Sky Nexus is a 2D pixel art action game set in a futuristic sci-fi universe. You'll battle through intense side-scrolling combat with retro-style visuals inspired by classic 16-bit games. It's an indie passion project bringing old-school gaming vibes to a new generation.

Why back this?

Support an indie developer bringing their creative vision to life. You get early access to a game that blends nostalgic pixel art with modern sci-fi action gameplay. Back now to help shape the game's development and be part of the journey from the ground up.

How does it work?

Choose your pledge tier to pre-order the game and unlock backer rewards. Your support funds the development - art, music, and gameplay features. Emily will share updates throughout development so you can watch Sky Nexus come to life.

What makes it special?

It combines beautiful retro pixel art aesthetics with intense sci-fi action in a futuristic universe full of dangers and secrets. Made with passion by an indie creator, not a big studio - your backing directly supports an artist chasing their dream.
