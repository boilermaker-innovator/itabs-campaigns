The Thundercats Roleplaying Game is Here!

What is it?

The official Thundercats Roleplaying Game - a 250-page hardcover book that lets you play as Lion-O, Cheetara, Panthro, or create your own Thunderian hero. Built for D&D 5e rules, it's packed with lore, mechanics, and adventures set on Third Earth.

Why back this?

This is the first official Thundercats RPG ever made. You get everything needed to run full campaigns - character stats for all your favorites, iconic locations, vehicles, weapons, and enemies like Mumm-Ra. Perfect for fans who grew up shouting "Thundercats Hooooo!"

How does it work?

Choose a pledge tier to pre-order your book. Base hardcover, limited edition with red accents, or premium leatherbound (only 1,985 copies to honor the 1985 debut). Ships February 2026 - the game is already complete and ready for printing.
